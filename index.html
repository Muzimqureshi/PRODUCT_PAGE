What is a drone?
A drone, also known as an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), is an aircraft operated without a human pilot on board. Drones are controlled remotely by a pilot or can be programmed to fly autonomously using GPS and other technologies.
Do I need a license to fly a drone?
In many countries, recreational drone pilots may not require a license if they follow specific rules and operate within designated areas. However, for commercial purposes or flying drones above a certain weight limit, obtaining a license or certification may be necessary.
How long can a drone fly on a single battery charge?
The flight time of a drone varies based on its size, weight, and the type of battery it uses. Consumer drones typically have flight times ranging from 10 to 30 minutes, while professional drones might stay airborne for up to 40 minutes or more.
What is the best drone for beginners?
For beginners, we recommend starting with a user-friendly and affordable drone with essential features like stability, ease of control, and safety features like propeller guards.
